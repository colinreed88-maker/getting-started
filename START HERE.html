Getting Started

AI at Flow

Welcome to Flow's AI development toolkit. This guide takes you from zero coding experience to building branded dashboards, presentations, and internal tools -- all with AI agents doing the heavy lifting.

We use two AI development tools: Cursor (an AI-powered code editor) and Claude (Anthropic's AI assistant). Together, they form a system where you describe what you want to build, and the AI writes the code, follows our brand standards, and deploys the result automatically.

This is not about becoming a software engineer. It is about giving Flow team members superpowers.

What is in the Config Folder

The config/ folder contains everything you need to configure your AI tools. Guide 03 walks you through how to install each piece.

Prerequisites

  • A Windows, Mac, or Linux computer with admin permissions
  • A GitHub account (free)
  • A Cursor license (check with your manager)
  • An Anthropic account for Claude (check with your manager)
  • Curiosity and patience -- the first setup takes a couple hours, then everything moves fast

Questions

If you get stuck at any point, ask your manager or the team channel, or open a conversation in Cursor or Claude and describe the problem. The AI agents are configured to help you debug.
